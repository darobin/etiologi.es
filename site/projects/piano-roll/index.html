One-Dimensional Avalanche Piano Roll

This project runs avalanches on one-dimensional, non-looping lattice, with varying parameters. At every time step (when a grain of sand of dropped on the pile), all the avalanches that take place mark a bar of their extend on a line beneath the lattice, and the line is moved down.

The idea is to see if it lends itself to some sort of analysis at that level.
